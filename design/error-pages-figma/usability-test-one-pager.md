# Error Recovery Usability One-Pager

## Scope

Optional follow-up if the team chooses to test the static frames before implementation.

## Session Plan

- Recommended count: `5` moderated sessions
- Audience: small-business operators, finance admins, or creators who use StreamPay to monitor recurring payments
- Session length: `20-25 minutes`

## Test Script

1. Show the `404` frame and ask: `What do you think happened here, and what would you do next?`
2. Show the `5xx` frame and ask: `Does this feel like your mistake or ours? What tells you that?`
3. Show the Stellar outage frame and ask: `How does this make you feel about your money or recent activity?`
4. Ask participants to find the main action and the backup action on each frame.
5. Ask whether any wording feels too technical, too vague, or too alarming.

## Success Signals

- Participants identify `404` as a bad or expired link.
- Participants identify `5xx` as a StreamPay-side issue.
- Participants understand that the Stellar outage is temporary.
- Participants feel reassured that funds are not gone, without interpreting the message as a legal guarantee.
- Participants can locate the primary action first.
