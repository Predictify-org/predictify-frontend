Usability session checklist — Streams search/filter (10–15m)

Before session
- Share Figma link and confirm platform (Zoom/Meet).
- Open `USABILITY_SCRIPT.md` and the prototype frames.

During session (10–15m)
1. Intro (1m): purpose, record consent, ask participant to think aloud.
2. Task (5–8m): "Find a paused stream for recipient 'Acme Corp' and narrow results to USD." Record start/end time and success.
3. Prompt if stuck (after 60s): ask "What would you try next?" Allow another 60s.
4. Debrief (3–5m): Ask: "Was it clear how to search by recipient?" "Do the status chips make sense?" "Any confusion on mobile?"

Metrics to capture
- Success: Yes/No
- Time to complete (seconds)
- One prioritized pain point
- Notes on unexpected behaviors

Post-session
- Add brief result line to `design/streams-search-filter/USABILITY_RESULTS.md` with participant id (P1), success, time, and top note.
- After 3 sessions, summarize success rate and top 3 pain points.

Consent & recording
- Ask for consent to record. If declined, take notes manually.

Optional: remote moderation tips
- Share screen only when necessary; let participant drive the prototype.
- Encourage think-aloud but don't coach the task.
