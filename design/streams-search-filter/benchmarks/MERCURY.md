Mercury — benchmark notes

What to capture
- Search placement: prominent top-left search, scoped to accounts/recipients.
- Chips: subtle pill chips with clear active state and good contrast.
- Density: compact list rows with clear primary and secondary text.

What to borrow
- Clear search scoping language (e.g., "Search recipients")
- Compact row density option for power users

What to avoid
- Overly heavy visual treatment for chips that obscures row data
